How it works

Up and running in 3 minutes

No code. No complicated setup. Just connect, describe, and automate.

01

Connect your tools

Link your apps with one click. Aria supports 150+ integrations including Slack, Gmail, Notion, Jira, GitHub, Salesforce, and more.

02

Describe what you need

Tell Aria what you want in plain language. It figures out the steps, asks clarifying questions if needed, and plans the workflow for you.
